Sort The Numbers Game

Score: 0

Wins: 0

Defeats: 0

The essence of the game: arrange the numbers in the fields in ascending order (from smallest to largest).
Numbers can be the same.
Add some fields for numbers (at least 3) using the "Add" button or delete them using the "Delete" button.
When you click on the "Start the game" button, the buttons are disabled, the bet will be made (numerically equal to the number of fields).
A number from 1 to 100 inclusive will appear in the lower window.
Click on any unoccupied field to enter this number there.
Repeat until you fill in all the fields. Then you will see your result.
If you win, your points increase according to the following formula: (number of fields)^2 -> (3^2=9, 4^2=16),
in case of defeat, the number of points will decrease by a number equal to the number of fields.
